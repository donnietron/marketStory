Farmer's Markets by State

As an avid proponent of farmer's markets I wanted to see if I could visualize how different states compared in their farmer's market landscapes.

Companion visualization available here. Market data from data.gov and census data from the census bureau.

Hover over the map or chart to see the state's path through different metrics. Click to keep highlighted for comparisons. I have preselected a few interesting groupings, but please explore your own and let me know anything interesting you find!
